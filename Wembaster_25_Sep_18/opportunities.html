    • SHPE Annual Events


  • Día de Ciencias -
    Día de Ciencias is an event for high school students and their parents made possible by the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers and its sponsors with the intent of increasing the enrollment of underrepresented high school students into higher education. The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers will present informational workshops that will familiarize both the students and their parents with college.
  • Engineers Got Talent! -
    An annual talent showcase put on by you! Talents can be musical, artistic, performance based... whatever you can do, as long as it's appropriate!
  • End of the Year Banquet -
    End the school year with a bang! Come join us for our annual themed banquet; one last fiesta with your SHPE familia including food, music, and our traditional piñata!
