Meet Our Graduates

Christian Alcaraz

Hey Everyone, My name is Christian Alcaraz. 4th year PhD Candidate in Dr. Charles Wyman's lab in the CEE department and co-graduate leader. My research focuses on biofuels, specifically turning agricultural waste into liquid fuel. I received my B.S in Chemical Engineering at UCR and have been an active SHPE member for the past three years. I am currently on the National Graduate Committee for SHPE where I work with other graduate students on national SHPE strategies. If you need help with resumes, study habits, interview questions, campus questions, life after graduate school I can certainly give you insights that have worked (and have not worked) for me in the past.

If my office hours do not work for you please feel free to contact me by phone or by email, (909) 6090-4789 calca005@ucr.edu.

Jeanette Cobian

Hi All, My name is Jeanette Cobian Iñiguez and I am a 4th year PhD student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering. I work in Dr. Princevac's lab where my focus is heat transfer and fluids with an emphasis in wildfire ignition and spread. I received my B.S. in mechanical engineering from UC San Diego and my M.S. in mechanical engineering from Cal State Los Angeles. At CSULA I was an LSAMP BD Fellow and at UC Riverside I am a FIELDS (NASA- JPL-UCR) Fellow. I have been a proud member of the SHPE Familia since 2009. Please reach out to me if you have questions on graduate school, scholarship, fellowship and research applications. If you have general questions on graduate school, research, careers in engineering, fire science, data science or if you want to practice your elevator pitch en español please don't hesitate on contacting me. We are one SHPE familia so if there is any insight I can share with you to help you succeed please let me know. Si Se Puede!

I can be reached by phone or by email: (619) 942-1021, jcobi002@ucr.edu. Feel free to make an appointment with me if my office hours do not work for you.
